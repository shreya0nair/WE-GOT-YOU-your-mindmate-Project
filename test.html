Some common mental health issues include:


1) Depression:persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and a loss of interest in activities once enjoyed.
2) Anxiety Disorders: cause excessive worry, fear, and nervousness.
3) Bipolar Disorder:fluctuations between depressive and manic episodes, with extreme mood swings.
4) Schizophrenia: cause hallucinations, delusionsand altered perceptions of reality.
5) Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD): after experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event, leading to flashbacks, nightmares, and heightened anxiety.
6) Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD): Characterized by intrusive, repetitive thoughts (obsessions).
7) Eating Disorders: involve unhealthy eating patterns and distorted body image.
8) Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD): affects attention, impulse control, and hyperactivity.
9) Substance Use Disorders: Involving the misuse of drugs or alcohol
